Schoology Grades Trend Visualizer

You can now view your infnite campus schedule and and see your grades' trend on Schoology!

Step 1: Login

Logout and log back into schoology.

Step 2: View grade trend

Go to your grade report and click on a course. At the bottom of your grades, you should see a graph of your grades over the grading period! Here is an example chart.

Step 3: View schedule

You can view your schedule from anywhere on schoology. Just click your image on the top right and click "schedule". If it is your first time on this page, it could take up to five seconds to load.



Step 4: Celebrate!

You can now be happy, because your schedule will show up in a beautiful format at schoology.harker.org/schedule and your grades will be graphed at schoology.harker.org/grades/grades.
